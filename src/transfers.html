⭐ Our Favourite Transfers

Five rumoured signings we think would improve Manchester United instantly.

Victor Osimhen ST

Osimhen would give United a true penalty-box striker. His pace, movement, physicality and pressing ability make him perfect for Premier League intensity. He turns half-chances into goals and provides a consistent attacking focal point.

Frenkie de Jong MID

De Jong brings composure and control in midfield. He can resist pressure, progress the ball, and dictate tempo. His intelligence and technical ability would help United dominate possession.

Jarrad Branthwaite CB

Branthwaite adds height, strength, and defensive stability. He improves set-piece defending, wins aerial duels, and brings calmness at the back — a long-term defensive pillar.

Diogo Costa GK

A modern keeper comfortable with distribution and high lines. Costa improves build-up play, stays calm under pressure, and offers reliable shot-stopping in big moments.

Michael Olise RW

Olise offers creativity and end product from the right. He can unlock low blocks with his passing/crossing and add consistent threat in the final third.
